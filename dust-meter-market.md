<p><ul><li>PM2.5 Dust Meter</li><li>PM10 Dust Meter</li><li>TSP(Total Suspended Particles) Dust Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dust meter market consists of various types, including PM2.5, PM10, and Total Suspended Particles (TSP) dust meters. PM2.5 dust meters specifically measure particles with a diameter of 2.5 micrometers or smaller, which are critical for assessing health impacts. PM10 dust meters capture particles up to 10 micrometers, indicating broader air quality concerns. TSP dust meters measure all airborne particles regardless of size, providing comprehensive air quality data. Each type serves specific regulatory, environmental, and health monitoring needs.</p></p>
