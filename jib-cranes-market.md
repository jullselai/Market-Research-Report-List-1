<p><p>The jib cranes market encompasses various types, including free-standing, mast type, and wall-mounted options. Free-standing jib cranes are anchored to the ground, providing flexibility and mobility for heavy loads. Mast type jib cranes are attached to a vertical mast, offering enhanced stability and a compact design, ideal for limited spaces. Wall-mounted jib cranes are fixed to walls, optimizing floor space and allowing for efficient overhead lifting in areas with restricted ground access, making them versatile for various industrial applications.</p></p>
