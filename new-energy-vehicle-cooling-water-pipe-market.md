<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) cooling water pipe market includes four main types: metal, rubber, nylon, and thermoplastic vulcanizates (TPV). Metal pipes offer durability and high-temperature resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Rubber pipes provide flexibility and excellent thermal insulation, ideal for various configurations. Nylon pipes are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, enhancing performance. TPV combines the benefits of rubber and thermoplastics, offering superior temperature tolerance and chemical resistance, making it a versatile choice for NEV cooling systems.</p></p>
