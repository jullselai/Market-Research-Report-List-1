<p><p>The medical grade sodium alginate market is categorized by viscosity levels, which dictate its applications in various medical and pharmaceutical fields. Low viscosity (below 150 mPa.s) is typically used for wound dressings and gut-health formulations due to its easy dispersibility. Medium viscosity (150–400 mPa.s) serves applications like dental impressions and controlled drug delivery systems, balancing thickness and fluidity. High viscosity (above 400 mPa.s) is essential for products requiring significant gel strength, such as tissue engineering scaffolds and advanced drug formulations.</p></p>
