<p><p>The Dynamic Data Management System (DDMS) market is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based systems. On-Premise solutions are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing enhanced control, security, and customization but often requiring higher initial investments and maintenance. Conversely, Cloud-Based systems are hosted on external servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness, with users paying based on usage. This flexibility supports remote collaboration and reduces the burden of system upkeep, catering to diverse organizational needs.</p></p>
