<p><ul><li>Single Channel Detectors</li><li>Multi-Channel Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared pyroelectric sensors are classified into single-channel and multi-channel detectors based on their functionality. Single-channel detectors are designed to detect infrared radiation from one source, making them ideal for applications like motion detection and occupancy sensing. In contrast, multi-channel detectors incorporate multiple sensing elements to capture signals from various sources simultaneously, offering enhanced sensitivity and accuracy. This versatility allows multi-channel detectors to be utilized in complex systems, such as environmental monitoring and advanced security applications, where detailed analysis of thermal changes is critical.</p></p>
