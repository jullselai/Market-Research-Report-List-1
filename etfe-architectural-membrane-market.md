<p><p>The ETFE architectural membrane market is categorized into three types: transparent, semi-transparent, and opaque. Transparent membranes allow maximum light transmission, making them ideal for structures requiring open brightness, like atriums. Semi-transparent membranes provide a balance, filtering light while maintaining privacy, suitable for spaces like commercial buildings. Opaque membranes, on the other hand, offer complete privacy and can be used for shading or aesthetic purposes in projects where light control is essential, such as facades or roofing systems.</p></p>
