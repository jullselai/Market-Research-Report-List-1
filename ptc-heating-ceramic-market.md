<p><p>The PTC Heating Ceramic market consists of various substrate types, including High Temperature Co-firing Multilayer Ceramic (HTCC) and Low Temperature Co-firing Multilayer Ceramic (LTCC). HTCC substrates are designed for high-temperature applications, offering excellent thermal stability and electrical performance, making them suitable for advanced electronics. In contrast, LTCC substrates enable co-firing at lower temperatures, allowing for greater versatility and integration in consumer electronics and automotive applications. The "Others" category encompasses additional ceramic types that cater to specific niche applications in the market.</p></p>
