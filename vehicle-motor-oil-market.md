<p><p>The vehicle motor oil market comprises three main types: ordinary motor oil, semi-synthetic oil, and synthetic oil. Ordinary motor oil, derived from crude oil, offers basic engine protection and is cost-effective. Semi-synthetic oil blends conventional oil with synthetic components, providing enhanced performance and protection. Synthetic oil, fully engineered from chemical compounds, offers superior lubrication, longevity, and resistance to breakdown, making it ideal for high-performance engines. Each type caters to different needs based on engine requirements and consumer preferences.</p></p>
