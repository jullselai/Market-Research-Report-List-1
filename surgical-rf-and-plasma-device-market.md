<p><p>The Surgical RF and Plasma Device market encompasses two primary types: RF devices and plasma devices. RF devices utilize radiofrequency energy to cut, coagulate, or ablate tissue, enhancing precision in surgical procedures. They are widely used in various specialties due to their effectiveness in minimizing thermal damage. Plasma devices, on the other hand, generate ionized gas to achieve similar surgical outcomes, providing unique benefits such as reduced bleeding and improved healing. Both types are integral to modern minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
