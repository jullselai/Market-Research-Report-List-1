<p><strong>The Plate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Plate Reader market primarily comprises two types: Optical Filter Microplate Readers and Optical Grating Microplate Readers. Optical Filter Microplate Readers utilize specific filters to isolate light wavelengths, making them efficient for monochromatic assays. They are generally simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, Optical Grating Microplate Readers employ diffraction gratings to disperse light, allowing for more versatile wavelength selection and higher sensitivity in assays. This makes them suitable for advanced applications requiring detailed spectral analysis. Both types cater to various research and clinical needs.</p></p>
