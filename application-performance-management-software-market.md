<p><p>Application Performance Management (APM) software is categorized into three market types: on-premises, cloud, and hybrid. On-premises APM is installed locally within a company's infrastructure, offering control and customization but requiring significant resources. Cloud APM is hosted on external servers, providing scalability and ease of access with lower upfront costs. Hybrid APM combines both on-premises and cloud solutions, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both environments, enhancing flexibility and performance monitoring capabilities across diverse infrastructures.</p></p>
