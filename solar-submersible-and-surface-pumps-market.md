<p><p>The solar submersible and surface pumps market consists of two primary types: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, efficiently transporting water from deep sources, making them ideal for wells and boreholes. In contrast, surface pumps are installed above ground and draw water from rivers, lakes, or tanks, suitable for irrigation and drainage applications. Both types leverage solar energy, providing cost-effective, eco-friendly solutions for various water management needs in agricultural, residential, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
