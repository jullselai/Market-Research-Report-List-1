<p><strong>The Hotel Booking Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hotel booking software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based systems are hosted on the provider's servers, allowing hotels to access services via the internet. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs, making it ideal for businesses seeking efficiency and ease of use. In contrast, premise-based systems are installed locally on hotel servers, giving the property full control over data and security. However, they require higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance efforts.</p></p>
