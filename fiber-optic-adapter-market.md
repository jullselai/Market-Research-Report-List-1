<p><p>The fiber optic adapter market includes several types, with SC, FC, and LC being the most common. SC (Standard Connector) adapters facilitate easy connections with a push-pull design, while FC (Ferrule Connector) adapters offer a screw-on mechanism, providing a secure connection for high-performance applications. LC (Lucent Connector) adapters are compact and widely used in high-density environments due to their reduced footprint. Other types may include MTP/MPO and ST connectors, catering to various industry needs and specifications.</p></p>
