<p><p>The carbon remover market is categorized into three main types: acidic, neutral, and alkaline carbon removers. Acidic carbon removers effectively target and dissolve carbon deposits in heavily soiled environments, making them suitable for industrial applications. Neutral carbon removers offer a versatile solution for everyday cleaning without damaging surfaces, ideal for household use. Alkaline carbon removers, on the other hand, excel in breaking down organic carbon compounds, often used in grease and oil removal for automotive and industrial purposes.</p></p>
