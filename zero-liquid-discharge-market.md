<p><p>The Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) market includes two main types: Conventional ZLD Systems and Hybrid ZLD Systems. Conventional ZLD Systems employ a comprehensive approach, utilizing evaporation and crystallization to eliminate liquid waste. These systems typically require significant energy and infrastructure investments. In contrast, Hybrid ZLD Systems combine various technologies, including membrane processes and evaporation, optimizing efficiency and reducing costs. Hybrid systems offer greater flexibility and adaptability to varying industrial processes while maintaining environmental compliance and resource recovery objectives.</p></p>
