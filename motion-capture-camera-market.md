<p><p>The motion capture camera market consists of several types, including optical, inertial, and other technologies. Optical systems utilize multiple cameras to track reflective markers placed on subjects, providing high accuracy for animation and film. Inertial systems rely on sensors that detect motion through acceleration and rotation, allowing for greater portability and less setup complexity. Other market types may include magnetic systems or hybrid approaches, combining features of both optical and inertial technologies to enhance tracking precision and versatility across various applications.</p></p>
