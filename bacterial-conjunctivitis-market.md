Macrolides target bacterial protein synthesis and are useful for those allergic to penicillin. Aminoglycosides, effective against gram-negative bacteria, are often used in severe cases. Each category plays a crucial role in addressing the prevalence of bacterial conjunctivitis in different patient demographics.</p></p>
