<p><strong>The Miniature Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS Type</li><li>CCD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The miniature cameras market comprises primarily two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras. CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them popular in smartphones and compact devices. In contrast, CCD cameras excel in image quality and low-light performance, making them favored in professional photography and medical imaging. Both types cater to various applications, including surveillance, drones, and automotive systems, significantly influencing the market dynamics.</p></p>
