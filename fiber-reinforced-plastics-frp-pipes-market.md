<p><p>Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) pipes are categorized primarily into Glass FRP (GFRP) and Carbon FRP (CFRP) types. GFRP pipes utilize glass fibers for enhanced strength and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for water and chemical transport applications. CFRP pipes, reinforced with carbon fibers, offer superior tensile strength and lightweight properties, suitable for high-stress environments like aerospace and automotive industries. Both types provide durability and maintenance advantages, driving their adoption in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
