<p><p>The brain monitoring sensors market encompasses various technologies, including dissolvable wireless sensors, wearable sensors, and implantable sensors. Wearable sensors, often in the form of headbands or caps, provide non-invasive monitoring for various applications, including sleep studies and cognitive assessments.
