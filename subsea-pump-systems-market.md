<p><p>The subsea pump systems market features various pump types tailored for offshore applications, including Helical Axial Flow Type and Twin Screw Type pumping systems. Helical Axial Flow pumps utilize a helical impeller to move fluids efficiently along a defined path, ideal for high flow rates. In contrast, Twin Screw pumps consist of two intermeshing rotors that create a sealed chamber for precise, continuous fluid transfer, making them suitable for handling viscous fluids and maintaining stable pressure in subsea environments.</p></p>
