<p><p>The IoT modules market encompasses various connectivity types tailored for different applications. Zigbee and Bluetooth/BLE support short-range, low-power connections for smart devices. Wi-Fi provides high-speed internet access, suitable for bandwidth-intensive applications. RFID enables automatic identification and tracking of objects. Each technology caters to specific needs, balancing factors like range, power consumption, and data transfer requirements in IoT deployments.</p></p>
