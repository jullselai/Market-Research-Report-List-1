<p><p>The scanning laser ophthalmoscope market is segmented into monocular and binocular ophthalmoscopes. Monocular ophthalmoscopes utilize a single lens for imaging, offering a compact design and cost-effectiveness, making them accessible for routine examinations. In contrast, binocular ophthalmoscopes feature dual lenses, providing a three-dimensional view of the retina and enhancing depth perception, which is beneficial for detailed assessments and diagnosis. Both types play crucial roles in ophthalmic diagnostics, catering to varying clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
