<p><p>The photodetectors market comprises various types, primarily including junction photodetectors and avalanche photodetectors. Junction photodetectors operate on the principle of p-n junctions, converting light into electrical signals, making them suitable for low-light applications. Avalanche photodetectors leverage the avalanche multiplication effect, enhancing sensitivity and enabling detection of weak light signals, ideal for telecommunications and fiber optics. Other types include photomultiplier tubes and metal-semiconductor-metal detectors, catering to specific industrial, medical, and research applications, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
